Rule

  1. Obtain rough location information of the check point.
  2. Use the items (location information obtained in step 3, gyro sensor and Bluetooth) to find the location of the check point.
  3. Scan the QR code posted at the check point.
  4. Solve the quiz.
  5. Repeat steps 3 to 6 as many times as the number of check points.

  • Do not run during the tour.
  • Do not share information with others because it will make it less interesting!
